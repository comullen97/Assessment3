This lovely community is settled right in Cougar Mountain, there are hundreds of hiking trails right outside your door to explore! There are also many community parks, and a lovely French themed cafe on Talus Road. We provide many sponsored neighborhood events for kids, adults, and familys. We take pride in reach out and serving our community.
Make sure to keep an eye out for bears, and HOA 'Karen' board members.
